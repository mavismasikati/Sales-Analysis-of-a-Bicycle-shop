# Sales-Analysis-of-a-Bicycle-shop
This repository contains a sales analysis of a bicycle shop using Excel and Power BI. It covers data cleaning, exploration, and visualization to uncover sales trends, product performance, and customer insights. The project demonstrates practical data analysis and dashboard creation for business decision-making.


#Project Overview

This project provides a comprehensive sales analysis of a global bicycle retailer using Excel and Power BI. The goal is to track key performance indicators (KPIs), identify growth opportunities, uncover profitability drivers, and deliver actionable insights for business decision-making.

The project demonstrates advanced skills in data cleaning, analysis, visualization, and dashboard development, highlighting the ability to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights.


#Key Achievements & Insights


Total Sales Performance: Tracked $118.73M in total sales with a profit margin of 14.23%.

Top Product Line: Paseo generated $33.01M, highlighting product-level revenue contribution.

Regional Growth: Japan contributed $12.55M to total sales in 2024, indicating a high-performing market.

Sales Volume: Over 1.13M units sold across all segments.


#Visualizations & Dashboards


Sales Trend Analysis: Month-over-month view highlighting seasonal fluctuations and growth patterns.

Customer Segmentation: Breakdown by Government, Small Business, and Enterprise levels to understand customer distribution.

Product Distribution Analysis: Comparison of Sales % vs Units Sold % to identify high-value vs high-volume items.

Decomposition Tree: AI-driven root-cause analysis to drill down into sales by category, helping identify performance drivers.


#Tools & Technologies Used


Power BI: Interactive dashboards, reporting, and advanced visualization techniques.

Excel: Data cleaning, aggregation, pivot tables, and preliminary analysis.

Data Analysis Skills Demonstrated: Data cleaning, KPI tracking, trend analysis, segmentation, and insights communication.


#Recommendations

Based on the analysis of bicycle shop sales data (2023-2024), the following recommendations are suggested for business improvement:

Focus on High-Performing Products:

Prioritize inventory and marketing for the Paseo product line, as it generates the highest revenue.

Target High-Growth Regions:

Expand marketing and distribution efforts in Japan, which showed strong sales performance.

Optimize Customer Segmentation:

Tailor strategies for Government, Small Business, and Enterprise customers to increase sales efficiency and retention.

Monitor Seasonal Trends:

Prepare for peak sales periods using insights from month-over-month sales trends to manage inventory and promotions effectively.

Leverage Data-Driven Decision Making:

Continue using dashboards and analytics to track KPIs, identify underperforming products, and improve profitability.
